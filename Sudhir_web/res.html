About Sudhir Shivaram


Sudhir Shivaram is much loved, recognized, and respected in wildlife photography circles not only in India but the world over. Engineer by education, photographer by passion, teacher by intuition, Sudhir is an inspiration to scores of people that aspire to break away from the mundane and chase their dreams.

Sudhir Shivaram is one of India’s most respected and renowned wildlife photographers. A long and lucrative career in the IT industry did not diminish Sudhir’s passion for photography but on the contrary, ensured that he never lost touch with his wild side. So much so that he bid adieu to the corporate jungle to spend more time in the real one.

Sudhir’s pictures are noteworthy for their technically sophisticated and evocative character. This green evangelist campaigns for wildlife protection around the world and gives freely of his time and images to raise awareness about nature and wildlife.

Sudhir was one of the brand ambassadors for Canon India and a Canon contract photographer. He recieved the Sanctuary Asia Wildlife Photographer of the Year 2012 award.

Asian Photography Cover Shot

What better way to announce Sudhir’s third Asian Photography award than carrying his image as cover shot for the Feb 2017 issue.
To elaborate a bit more on the Asian Photography awards – it is the most coveted award in the Indian photography industry, where the most influential people including CEOs, managers and business heads of top companies (such as Canon, Nikon, Sony, HP, and the like); photography institutes; and individual photographers are nominated. To ensure that the whole process remains impartial and unbiased, jury members are picked from outside of the industry. The members evaluate the nominees and their contributions based on a wide range of parameters and score them accordingly.

As per the xxlpix website, the Web Popularity Index (WPI) takes into consideration the three key factors of the social web: reach, interaction with users, and external references. Thousands of social media and web indicators for popular photographers of the present day were collected during the survey period and evaluated for the creation of the index.
We analysed: fans, followers and subscribers on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest, Flickr, Tumblr, 500px and other social networks, ratios for interaction with subscribers, fans and followers, as well as references for the photographers’ publications.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Q1. Does the advanced plan include everything present in the basic plan or is it completely different?

The advanced plan includes everything in the basic plan and more:

Benefits include,

  • Access to 5 extra advanced video courses.
  • Getting your photography-related queries answered.
  • Getting equipment-related advice and guidance.
  • Having any queries from your online tutorials clarified.
  • Getting critique on your images by sharing it with the community.

Q2. What’s the difference between what you teach inside the course and the thousands of free Photography videos found on sites like Youtube?

Great question. The courses we’ve created here for our students are structured keeping in mind the journey of a photography student. With 25+ years of photography experience, I’ve specially designed it to help students discover their inherent photography skills and fine-tune it with the help of our learner’s group and experts. The course material is much more in-depth than the usual tips and tricks you find on the internet. Besides, free courses only teach you the theoretical aspects of photography, but here at SSP, we lay a special emphasis on task evaluation, feedback sharing and group learning.

Q3. Are your courses available in Hindi?

At this time, all our courses are available only in the English language.

Q4. Can I get only one of your courses instead?

To purchase individual courses, hop on this link and that’ll take you to Amazon website where you can buy the courses individually.

Q5. Do you provide any certification on the completion of tutorials?

Good question. The short answer is No, we don’t. The reason – we believe that learning is a continuous process and that your practical skills matter more than any certification.

Q6. Is my camera supported in this course? What if I have a different camera?

I’m glad you asked. The point is, you can learn to develop most of your foundational photography skills with the camera you already have right now. For advanced students, we do provide specific training for specific cameras. But if you have a different camera, the foundational techniques are still covered in the other lessons and a different camera wouldn’t stop you from building your skills.

Q7. I am interested in your courses. But in your videos, I see that you use a lot of high-end equipment. Do I need to buy new equipment before joining your courses?

I’m glad you’re interested in building your skills further. The way we’ve structured the program is that you can start learning to take “magazine-worthy” photographs right now with the equipment you already have.

Both of our plans cover fundamental modules which include lessons on:

– Camera & Lens buying guide

– Basics & Advanced Fundamentals of Photography

– Post Processing Using Adobe Camera Raw and Photoshop

– Back button focusing

– Post processing using Capture NX-D

– Post Processing using Canon DPP

– Bird Photography

Lessons from these modules apply to any DSLR or modern point and shoot digital cameras. Still unsure or have a specific concern that’s holding you back? Drop us a line here.

Q8. Do you conduct any live trainings and workshops?

Absolutely. I conduct frequent photography tours and workshops with students and enthusiasts. To know further visit our Workshops Page and drop us a line.

Q9. How can I get the course details like timings, overall structure and things to carry?

Since this is an online course, you can learn at your own pace with what you already have right now. For any specific query drop us a line on our support section.

Q10. Which genre of photography can I learn from your courses?

The concepts you’ll be learning in the course will benefit you no matter what genre you wish to pursue in the future. However, we do cover an extensive range of genres, like landscape, nature, portrait, sports, wildlife, street and macro photography.

Q11. Do you have an EMI option?

At this time we don’t support the EMI option. You’ll have to pay the full course fee up-front.

Q12. What happens when I sign up to your photography course?

Once you join us, you’ll get access to our extensive library of 300+ photography training videos to learn from, at your own pace. Apart from that, you’ll also be invited into our exclusive FB community to enjoy membership benefits which include:

  1.  Supportive feedback/critiques on your images from fellow photographers and me
  2. Opportunity to participate in group discussions with ambitious and like-minded photographers
  3. Have your photography related questions answered by me or others in the community
  4. Participate in monthly theme-based photography assignments and if your photo is shortlisted it’ll be displayed in the EDITOR’s album
  5. Exclusive LIVE FB webinars from me only for this group where I will interact with you and discuss topics related to photography, answer your queries in real time and review pics to provide feedback.
  6. Use this group to Post your buy/sell requirements.
  7. Form groups with folks from your city and go for weekend shoots.

Q13. What if I don’t like it? Can I get a refund?

The course fee and any other payments are non-refundable. I understand that this is a valid concern for many of you. From the past, I’ve realised that there are mainly two reasons most folks have this concern – Either you don’t trust me or you don’t trust yourself to grow. In either case, I recommend going through the testimonials of previous students here and taking a decision based on that. No matter what, I’d love to support you in your journey as a photographer.

Q14. I tried to purchase but I received an error message?

Ah! That sucks. We’re really sorry about that. Please visit our support page and drop in your query and we’ll get it resolved asap.

Q15. Do I need any specific device or browser to view your courses?

Not at all. The course material can be accessed on any device (PC/Mac) or smartphone or tablet and using any browser.
